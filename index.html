Questions?

Frequently Asked

How long does it take to build a website?

Most websites are delivered within 7-14 days. Complex projects with custom features may take 3-4 weeks. We'll give you an exact timeline after our discovery call.

What's included in the price?

Everything! Design, development, mobile responsiveness, basic SEO, contact forms, and 30 days of free maintenance. Domain and hosting are separate but we'll help you set them up.

Do you provide content and images?

We can help with basic copywriting and use high-quality stock images. For professional photography or extensive copywriting, we can recommend partners or work with your team.

Can I update the website myself?

Yes! We provide training on how to update content. For more complex changes, we offer affordable monthly maintenance packages starting at just ₹999/month.

What about hosting and domain?

We'll guide you through purchasing a domain (₹500-1000/year) and hosting (₹2000-5000/year). We can manage this for you or help you set it up independently.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We typically work with 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments that work for your budget.
